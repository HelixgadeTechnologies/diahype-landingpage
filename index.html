About Us

Revitalize Your Health Today

DiaHype is a leading international health company based in Port Harcourt, Nigeria. With a dedicated team of experts in Nigeria, UK and USA, we strive to provide innovative solutions and services to promote a healthier lifestyle.


Our mission is to empower individuals to take control of their health and well-being through our comprehensive range of products and services.


Our flagship health interventions focus on prevention, early diagnosis and management of risk factors of Cardiovascular Diseases, especially Hypertension, Diabetes and Chronic Kidney Disease.
